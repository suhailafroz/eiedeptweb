Infrastructural Facilities


Mitsubishi Electric India Pvt. Ltd has established Authorized Training Center at the Department of EIE, CVRCE which is one among 4 Authorized Training Centers across INDIA, where training needs of local industries can be catered along with student skill development.

They have also sponsored Mitsubishi Robotic established worth around Rs. 35 Lakhs. It serves as a great opportunity for students to learn the basics of Advanced Robotics Supervisory control programming & methodologies with this Robotic Setup.
