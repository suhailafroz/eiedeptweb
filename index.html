Message from Head of the Department

The department of EIE was started in Academic year 2004-05 with an intake of 60 B.Tech. students. The department has one Emeritus Professor, two Professors, three Associate Professors, one Senior Asst. Professor and six Assistant Professors. The department has several advanced instruments to train students to meet the requirements of industry. A few among them are Lab VIEW software with Compact field module, Programmable Logic Controller (PLC) with several hardware modules, Process stations, Analytical instruments and Biomedical instruments. The department actively encourages students to take up practical experiments beyond the syllabus.

Vision and Mission

Vision

To turn out state of the art technologists in the area of Instrumentation engineering capable of meeting the challenging needs of industry in instrumentation and to create a ‘Centre of Excellence’ in Virtual Instrumentation.

Mission

  • M1: To provide the right blend of theory and practice thereby enabling students to become true professionals’ with high ethical values.
  • M2: To provide high quality education which allows students to realize their aspiration and potential.
  • M3: To improve knowledge, create passion for learning, foster innovation and nurture talents towards serving the society and country.
  • M4: To pursue research and consultancy service and provide the necessary rubric for SME’s (Small Medium Enterprise’s) development.
  • M5: Holistic development of the students and staff – The first and foremost mission.




Department Highlights

  • The Department has the Institutional Life Membership of ISTE, IETE. and faculty membership of IEEE society.
  • The Department received three projects (HMI based centralized water distribution system for metro cities, detecting poisonous gases in sewage lines, and smart traffic signaling System) from DST, Govt. of India (NSTEDB) New-Gen IEDC Programme with a sanctioned fund of Rs. 2, 50,000 worth of Rs 7.5 lakhs and successfully completed.
  • Department received a grant of rupees 16,30,000 in 2018-19 from AICTE for modernization of PC based Advanced Process Control Laboratory and its infrastructure development.
  • The department is actively involved in the development of SMART TRAFFIC SIGNALLING SYSTEM with Mr. G. Venkateswarlu, as the principal investigator. The project provides a centralized platform for providing a collaborative research and development environment with faculty and students.
  • The Department is conducting several short-term courses, Faculty Development Programs, workshops and guest lectures for the benefit of External and Internal faculty members and students under ISTE Chapter and Department Students Association (Instroglitz). The Department encourages the faculty members to enhance their knowledge by attending the Faculty Development Programs and Short Term Courses conducted by other reputed institutions.
  • EIE Department is an Authorized Training Center of Mitsubishi Electric Pvt Ltd. with a Robotic kit worth around 35 lakhs
